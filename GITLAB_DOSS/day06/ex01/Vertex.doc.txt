<- Vertex ----------------------------------------------------------------------
The Vertex class handles Vertex.

The vertex is very simple. It is used to encapsulate the vortex's coordinates
and gives reading and writing rights to its attributes.

An insctance can be constructed as:
Vertex(x: -.--, y:-.--, z:-.--, w:-.--)
or:
Vertex(x: -.--, y:-.--, z:-.--, w:-.--, Color())
(cf Color class).
